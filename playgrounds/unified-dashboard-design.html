Interaction Patterns

Navigation: Single sidebar with 6 main sections. Active state highlighted with background color and border. Badges show counts/status.

Hover Effects: Cards lift 2px on hover with shadow increase. Buttons have 1px lift. Quick action icons change color.

Progressive Disclosure: Complex actions hidden behind "View All" links. Details in modal panels.

Real-time Updates: Progress bars animate with shimmer effect. Resource bars update smoothly. Toast notifications appear bottom-right.

Feedback: All actions have visual feedback. Success states green. Errors red with context. Warnings yellow.
